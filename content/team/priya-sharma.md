Priya Sharma leads all program development and delivery at Global Women TechLeaders, including mentorship programs, leadership training, and educational initiatives.

## Background

Priya brings a unique combination of technology and education expertise to her role:

- 10+ years in learning & development at tech companies
- Former software engineer turned educator
- Published author on women in STEM education

## Key Initiatives

Under Priya's leadership, GWTL has launched several flagship programs:

### Global Top Tech Mentoring Program
A structured mentorship program connecting emerging leaders with senior executives, now in its 5th year with 500+ successful matches.

### Girls Who Lead
An Erasmus+ funded initiative empowering teenage girls with technology and leadership skills.

### Executive Leadership Certificate
A partnership with European Business University offering accredited leadership education.
