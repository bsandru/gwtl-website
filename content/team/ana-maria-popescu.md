Ana-Maria Popescu is the Founder and CEO of Global Women TechLeaders (GWTL), an organization she established in 2015 with a mission to accelerate gender parity in the technology sector.

## Background

With over two decades of experience in the technology industry, Ana-Maria has held leadership positions at major tech companies and startups across Europe and the United States. Her career spans software engineering, product management, and executive leadership.

## Vision for GWTL

Under Ana-Maria's leadership, GWTL has grown from a small networking group to a global organization with:

- 50,000+ members across 80 countries
- Partnerships with Fortune 500 companies
- Recognition by major international bodies

## Awards & Recognition

- European Women in Tech Award, 2023
- Forbes 50 Over 50, Europe, 2022
- Luxembourg Tech Leader of the Year, 2021

## Speaking Engagements

Ana-Maria is a sought-after speaker on topics including women in leadership, technology innovation, and organizational diversity. Recent appearances include:

- World Economic Forum, Davos
- Web Summit, Lisbon
- SXSW, Austin
