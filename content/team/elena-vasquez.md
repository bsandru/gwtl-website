Elena Vasquez serves as the Chief Operating Officer of Global Women TechLeaders, where she oversees the organization's global operations, partnerships, and program delivery.

## Professional Background

Before joining GWTL, Elena spent 15 years in operations and strategy roles at technology companies, including:

- Director of Operations at a European unicorn startup
- Management Consultant at McKinsey & Company
- Operations Manager at Google

## Role at GWTL

As COO, Elena is responsible for:

- **Global Operations**: Managing teams across multiple continents
- **Partnership Development**: Building relationships with corporate partners
- **Program Excellence**: Ensuring high-quality delivery of all GWTL initiatives
- **Organizational Strategy**: Driving growth while maintaining mission focus

## Education

- MBA, INSEAD
- BS Computer Science, ETH Zurich
