## News: Global Women TechLeaders Partners with Tech Show Frankfurt 2026

We are excited to announce that Global Women TechLeaders is an official partner of Tech Show Frankfurt, held at Messe Frankfurt on 6-7 May 2026.

As the region’s technology community gathers to discuss AI, data, cloud, and cybersecurity, our organization will be actively participating in the *"Empowered at Tech Show Frankfurt"* initiative. Since 2023, this program has served as a vital platform for women, allies, and industry leaders to share experiences and drive meaningful change toward a more inclusive future in tech.

Representing our organization, **Laura Moritz (Country Director Germany)** will join two distinct panels on **7 May** to discuss the intersection of culture, diversity, and technical evolution.

## Our Featured Panels

### Global Perspectives on Inclusion

**Time:** 11:50 - 12:35  
**Theatre:** Data Centre World Keynote  
**The Discussion:** A deep dive into how inclusive leadership acts as a catalyst for innovation and long-term impact.  
**Participants:** Laura Moritz will be joined by representatives from Women in DevOps and Women in Tech - Global Movement, moderated by Peter Mousaferiadis (Cultural Infusion).

### The Next Generation Data Team: Roles, Skills & Culture

**Time:** 14:30 - 15:15  
**Theatre:** Data & AI Engineering  
**The Discussion:** Exploring the shifting landscape of data engineering and the cultural foundation required to build high-performing, diverse data teams.  
**Participants:** Laura Moritz joins experts from Urban Sports Club, NHS England, and PPA LeaderTek.

## Networking & Connection

In addition to the stage discussions, the event offers dedicated space for the community to connect. Following the sessions on Day 2, we invite you to join us for informal networking and drinks in the *Visionärs Lounge*.

Whether you are a founder, technologist, or ally, we look forward to exchanging ideas and contributing together to a more representative tech industry.

For full details on the event and conference streams, visit the [Tech Show Frankfurt website](https://www.techshowfrankfurt.de/conference/content-streams).
