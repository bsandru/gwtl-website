### **Championing Women in Tech at the World’s Largest Economic Forum**

This year, **Global Women TechLeaders (GWTL)** had the privilege of being represented at the **World Economic Forum in Davos**, one of the most influential gatherings of global leaders. As part of **Davos Innovation Week**, our CEO took the stage at the **“Decoding the Invisible Ceiling in Tech”** panel, hosted by **QuantumfAI**.

### **Key Highlights from Davos 2025**

🌍 **Panel Discussion:** Our CEO joined industry leaders **Virginie Guignard Legros, Désirée Dosch, Valentina Ion, and Deniz Erkus, CFA** to discuss **real, actionable strategies to increase the presence of women in tech leadership.**\
🏆 **Celebrating Women in Innovation:** It was inspiring to see **Teodora Moraru** win the **Startup Innovation Award**for her startup, **Admin Tools**—a recognition of the incredible impact women-led startups can achieve.\
🔍 **The Reality Check:** Despite progress, women remain underrepresented in leadership and startup funding at major tech events.

### **Looking Ahead to Davos 2026**

While we celebrate the achievements of women in tech, we also recognize the **need for stronger representation, more allies, and systemic change**. Our goal for Davos 2026 is clear:

✅ More women-led startups at the innovation forefront\
✅ Stronger industry collaborations for gender equity in tech\
✅ A balanced leadership ecosystem where women and men work together to drive change

Change doesn’t happen in isolation. It requires **collaboration, advocacy, and continuous action**. **At GWTL, we are committed to making this vision a reality.**
