In a landmark event for women in technology, Global Women TechLeaders partnered with the **Romanian Parliament's Committee for Labour** to host a high-level roundtable discussion on advancing women's leadership in the tech sector.

## Event Theme

**"Shaping the Future: Tech Talent, Leadership & Startup Investment for Women"**

This roundtable brought together policymakers, industry leaders, and advocates to address systemic barriers and identify actionable solutions.

## Key Discussion Points

### Tech Talent Pipeline

- Educational initiatives to encourage girls in STEM
- Retention strategies for women in tech careers
- The role of mentorship and sponsorship

### Leadership Development

- Breaking the glass ceiling in tech companies
- Board representation and executive pathways
- Leadership training programs and their effectiveness

### Startup Investment

- Current statistics on funding for women-led startups
- Investor biases and how to address them
- Policy mechanisms to increase investment equity

## Outcomes

The roundtable resulted in several concrete commitments:

1. A working group to develop policy recommendations
2. Pledge from participating companies to improve diversity metrics
3. Plans for a follow-up summit to track progress

## Participating Organizations

- Romanian Ministry of Digital Transformation
- Major tech companies operating in Romania
- Romanian Startup Association
- European Women in Tech network
- Academic institutions focused on technology

## Media Coverage

The event received significant media attention, helping to elevate the conversation about women in technology to the national level.
