Global Women TechLeaders is proud to announce a strategic partnership with the **European Business University of Luxembourg (EBU)**, one of Europe's leading institutions for executive education.

## Partnership Overview

This collaboration will create new pathways for women in technology to access world-class leadership education while building a stronger network of women leaders across Europe.

## Key Initiatives

### Executive Leadership Certificate

A joint certificate program combining:

- EBU's rigorous academic curriculum
- GWTL's practical industry insights
- Networking opportunities with women leaders

### Research Collaboration

Joint research projects focusing on:

- Women's leadership in technology
- Best practices for organizational diversity
- Impact measurement of leadership programs

### Scholarship Fund

Together, we're establishing a scholarship fund to ensure financial barriers don't prevent talented women from accessing these opportunities.

## Program Benefits

Participants in the joint programs will receive:

- **Academic Excellence**: Courses designed and delivered by EBU faculty
- **Industry Relevance**: Real-world case studies and projects
- **Network Access**: Connection to GWTL's global community
- **Career Support**: Dedicated career services and mentorship

## Timeline

- **Q1 2025**: Program design and curriculum development
- **Q2 2025**: First cohort applications open
- **Q3 2025**: Program launch
- **2026**: Expansion to additional tracks
