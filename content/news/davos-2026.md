Global Women TechLeaders is proud to co-organize **Davos Innovation Week 2026**, bringing together thought leaders, executives, and innovators from around the world to discuss the future of leadership in an AI-driven era.

## Event Highlights

This year's program features two executive panels curated by GWTL:

### Panel 1: AI-Driven Leadership in the Digital Age

Our first panel explores how artificial intelligence is reshaping leadership paradigms across industries. Speakers include:

- Leading AI researchers from top tech companies
- Fortune 500 executives implementing AI strategies
- Policy makers shaping AI governance

### Panel 2: Investment Trends in Women-Led Tech Startups

The second panel focuses on the investment landscape for women-led tech ventures, examining:

- Current funding patterns and opportunities
- Success stories from women founders
- Strategies for closing the funding gap

## Why This Matters

As we navigate an increasingly complex global landscape, the need for diverse leadership perspectives has never been more critical. GWTL continues to champion women in technology leadership, ensuring their voices are heard at the highest levels of global discourse.

Register for Davos Innovation Week 2026 to be part of these groundbreaking conversations. Whether you're an investor, entrepreneur, or corporate leader, this event offers invaluable insights into the future of technology and leadership.
