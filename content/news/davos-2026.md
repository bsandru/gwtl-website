Global Women TechLeaders is proud to co-organize **Davos Innovation Week 2026**, bringing together thought leaders, executives, and innovators from around the world to discuss the future of leadership in an AI-driven era.

### **Women in Tech & Leadership — Davos Innovation Week 2026**

**Leadership That Keeps Pace With Innovation**

As AI reshapes industries and rewrites operating models, leadership must evolve. Not reactively, but intentionally. Not cautiously, but responsibly.

Our dedicated **Women in Tech & Leadership** track brings together senior executives, investors, and ecosystem leaders for conversations focused on:

- responsible innovation
- long‑term value creation
- leadership readiness in the age of AI
- the intersection of technology, trust, and human values

This is where global perspectives meet actionable insight.

## Event Highlights

This year's program features two executive panels curated by GWTL:

### **Panel 1: Shaping the Future: Leadership, Innovation & Trust in the Age of AI Transformation**

AI is advancing at unprecedented speed. The leaders who will thrive are those who can pair technological ambition with human values, trust, and sustainable impact. This high‑energy, candid conversation explores how organisations — and technologies — can scale responsibly and meaningfully in an AI‑driven world.
