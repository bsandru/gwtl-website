We are thrilled to announce the launch of **Girls Who Lead**, a transformative 24-month initiative funded by the Erasmus+ program, designed to empower young women aged 16-18 with the skills they need to become tomorrow's leaders.

## Program Overview

Girls Who Lead addresses a critical gap in leadership development for young women. Through a comprehensive curriculum that combines digital literacy, entrepreneurial thinking, and leadership training, participants will:

- **Develop Digital Skills**: Learn coding fundamentals, data analysis, and digital marketing
- **Build Entrepreneurial Mindset**: Create business plans, understand market dynamics, and pitch ideas
- **Cultivate Leadership Abilities**: Practice public speaking, team management, and strategic thinking

## Our Partner Organizations

This initiative brings together leading organizations from across Europe:

- Education technology experts
- Youth development organizations
- Business and entrepreneurship coaches
- Policy advocacy groups

## Timeline

The program runs from December 2025 through December 2027, with:

- **Q1 2026**: Participant selection and onboarding
- **Q2-Q4 2026**: Core training modules
- **2027**: Advanced workshops and mentorship matching
- **December 2027**: Graduation and alumni network launch

## How to Get Involved

We're looking for:

1. **Young women** aged 16-18 interested in technology and leadership
2. **Mentors** with experience in tech, business, or leadership roles
3. **Partner organizations** committed to youth empowerment

Contact us to learn more about participation opportunities.
