We are excited to announce the launch of our flagship **Global Top Tech Mentoring Program 2025**, designed to connect aspiring women technologists with experienced leaders in the field.

## Program Structure

The 2025 program has been redesigned based on feedback from previous cohorts to maximize impact and engagement.

### Duration & Format

- **6-month commitment** (March - August 2025)
- **Bi-weekly 1:1 sessions** between mentor and mentee
- **Monthly group workshops** on professional development topics
- **Quarterly networking events** for all participants

### Focus Areas

Mentees can choose from specialized tracks:

1. **Technical Leadership** - For engineers aspiring to lead teams
2. **Product Management** - For those transitioning to or growing in PM roles
3. **Entrepreneurship** - For future founders and startup leaders
4. **Executive Track** - For senior professionals targeting C-suite positions

## Who Should Apply?

### As a Mentee

- Women in tech with 2+ years of experience
- Committed to their professional development
- Ready to invest time in the mentorship relationship

### As a Mentor

- 10+ years in technology or related fields
- Leadership experience at manager level or above
- Passion for supporting women's advancement in tech

## Application Process

1. Complete the online application
2. Participate in a brief matching interview
3. Review and accept your mentor/mentee match
4. Begin your journey!

## Success Stories

Our program has facilitated over 500 successful mentoring relationships since 2020, with measurable impacts:

- 73% of mentees report career advancement within 12 months
- 89% of participants would recommend the program
- Alumni network spanning 40+ countries

## Apply Now

Applications for the 2025 cohort are open until February 15, 2025. Don't miss this opportunity to grow with the support of our incredible community.
