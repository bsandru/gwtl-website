## News: Global Women TechLeaders Partners with WeAreDevelopers 2026

We are proud to partner with **WeAreDevelopers**, the global platform where developers and AI professionals grow, connect, and lead in the age of AI.

Millions of professionals use the year-round platform to build skills, access thousands of hours of expert content, discover career opportunities, and engage with a global community sharing knowledge at scale. For companies, WeAreDevelopers offers an authentic way to connect with developer audiences, strengthen employer brand visibility, and showcase products to highly relevant tech talent.

## Global Events in 2026

The WeAreDevelopers flagship events take place across Europe, North America, and India, bringing together leading engineers, tech leaders, and AI professionals.

### WeAreDevelopers World Congress Europe

- **Location:** Berlin, Germany
- **Dates:** 8-10 July 2026

### WeAreDevelopers World Congress North America

- **Location:** San Jose, California, USA
- **Dates:** 23-25 September 2026

### WeAreDevelopers Conference India

- **Location:** Bengaluru, India
- **Dates:** 25-26 November 2026

## Why This Matters for Our Community

With more than **15,000 developers, AI professionals, and tech leaders** attending, WeAreDevelopers World Congress is widely recognized as one of the most important global gatherings for the developer community.

Participants can engage in:

- 500+ speakers and 500+ hours of expert content
- Keynotes, workshops, and masterclasses
- Hands-on sessions and deep-dive technical tracks
- A large-scale tech expo with 200+ partners
- Networking with 8,000 companies and 2,000 decision-makers

Content spans practical and strategic topics including **AI, Cloud, DevOps, Software Architecture, Frontend, UX/UI, Cybersecurity, Blockchain, IoT, and Automotive**.

Past speakers include globally recognized tech leaders such as **Steve Wozniak, Sir Tim Berners-Lee, and Thomas Dohmke**.
