Global Women TechLeaders made a significant impact at the **WeAreDevelopers World Congress 2025** in Berlin, one of the largest developer conferences in the world.

## Our Presence

GWTL hosted a dedicated track focusing on **Women Shaping AI**, bringing together industry leaders, researchers, and emerging talents to discuss critical topics at the intersection of gender diversity and artificial intelligence.

## Key Sessions

### Inclusive Innovation in AI Development

This session explored how diverse teams create better AI products:

- Case studies from leading tech companies
- Research findings on bias in AI systems
- Practical strategies for building inclusive teams

### AI Ethics: A Women's Perspective

Women leaders in AI ethics shared their insights on:

- Fairness and accountability in AI systems
- Regulatory frameworks and self-governance
- The role of diversity in ethical AI development

### Emerging Technologies and Career Opportunities

A forward-looking session helping attendees understand:

- The evolving AI job market
- Skills needed for AI careers
- Pathways for women entering the field

## Impact

The GWTL track attracted over 2,000 attendees across all sessions, with particularly strong engagement from early-career developers seeking to understand how they can contribute to more inclusive technology development.
