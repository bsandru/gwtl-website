## GWTL @ WeAreDevelopers World Congress Europe 2026

GWTL joins one of the world's most influential developer gatherings — the WeAreDevelopers World Congress Europe 2026 — in Berlin, Germany. Over three days, our delegation of women tech leaders connects with 15,000+ top-tier peers and 500+ global experts, building relationships and visibility at the heart of the global tech community.

### About WeAreDevelopers World Congress

WeAreDevelopers World Congress is the flagship event for developers and tech leaders across Europe. The 2026 edition takes place July 8–10 in Berlin, bringing together engineers, founders, CTOs, and industry innovators for three days of hands-on learning, keynotes, expo floor discovery, and peer networking at scale.
