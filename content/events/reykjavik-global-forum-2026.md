## GWTL @ Reykjavík Global Forum 2026

GWTL joins the 9th Annual Reykjavík Global Forum — the flagship event of the Reykjavík Global community and one of the world's most influential gatherings of women leaders. Co-hosted with the Government and Parliament of Iceland, the Forum brings together 500 invited leaders from over 80 countries for three days of masterclasses, panel discussions, workshops, roundtables, and curated dinners.

### About the Forum

Launched in 2018 under the theme "Power, Together!", the Reykjavík Global Forum – Women Leaders convenes women leaders and their allies from politics, business, civil society, academia, the arts, and media. Participants share ideas, spark solutions, and drive progress toward equality and increased women's representation in leadership positions.

The 2026 edition runs November 15–17 in Reykjavik under the theme **Power, Together for Community** — the most solution-driven programme yet, focused on Women in Leadership.

### Programme Tracks

Content is shaped around the Reykjavík initiatives and the Forum's core programme tracks:

- **Economic Growth**
- **Inclusive Innovation**
- **Research & Health**
- **Shared Responsibility**
- **Policy & Law**
- **Sustainable Stability**

### Who Should Attend

- Women leaders across politics, business, civil society, academia, the arts, and media
- Allies committed to advancing gender parity in leadership
- GWTL members and corporate partners seeking global visibility and meaningful dialogue
- Conversationists ready to listen deeply, share insights, and shape actionable outcomes

## Event Details

- **Dates:** November 15–17, 2026
- **Location:** Reykjavik, Iceland (Harpa Conference Centre area)
- **Attendees:** 500 invited global leaders from 80+ countries
- **Format:** Masterclasses, panels, workshops, roundtables, curated dinners
- **Host:** Co-hosted with the Government and Parliament of Iceland
