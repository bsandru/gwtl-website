GWTL Power Tables Berlin brings together senior decision-makers, investors, and women tech leaders for an evening of structured, outcome-driven conversations. Held the night before WeAreDevelopers World Congress, this invite-only gathering is designed to create real commitments — not just connections.

## Details

- **Format:** Structured conversations, Power Tables
- **Model:** GWTL 2.0 — every session is outcome-designed

## What to Expect
The Berlin Power Table is GWTL's first of 2026 — an invite-only gathering the evening before WeAreDevelopers World Congress opens. No keynotes. No panels. A skilled moderator, a room of people with real authority, and a structured session that moves from context to public commitment in a single evening.
