## Empowered at Tech Show Frankfurt: Driving Inclusion in Tech

On 6-7 May 2026, Tech Show Frankfurt returns to Messe Frankfurt, bringing together the region's technology community across AI, data, cloud, cybersecurity, and infrastructure.

As part of the programme, Empowered at Tech Show Frankfurt continues its mission to champion diversity, inclusion, and representation within the tech industry. Since 2023, the initiative has created a platform for women, allies, and industry leaders to come together, share experiences, and drive meaningful change.

In 2026, the keynote session on "She Builds, She Leads, She Inspires: Empowered by Tech" (7 May) will bring together leading voices from organisations such as adidas, Microsoft, Munich Re, and Frankfurt International School to explore how inclusive leadership drives innovation and long-term impact.

Beyond the stage, the initiative creates space for connection through dedicated networking moments, including informal drinks in the Visionars Lounge following the session on Day 2.

Whether you're a founder, technologist, leader, or ally, Empowered at Tech Show Frankfurt offers a space to connect, exchange ideas, and contribute to a more inclusive future in tech.
